The Concept

A Symphony of Grain & Gastronomy

At Nincasa, we have reimagined the craft dining experience. Named in honor of Ninkasi, the ancient Sumerian goddess of beer, our venue features a fully operating, state-of-the-art artisanal brewery in the heart of our dining hall.

Every plate that leaves our kitchen is designed to dialogue with a pour from our copper maturation tanks. Guests enjoy exclusive access to limited-batch brews, custom food pairings, and a sophisticated lounge atmosphere detailed in brushed brass and slate.

Direct Tank Taps

Freshly crafted beers piped directly to our main taproom bar at optimal temperatures.

Artisanal Pairings

Every menu item is paired with a specific house brew to elevate contrasting flavors.
